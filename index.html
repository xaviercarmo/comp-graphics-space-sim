A medium-weight fighter optimised to balance maneuverability, durability, and attack power. Has two guns positioned near the front-center of the ship, each with a moderate rate of fire with moderate damage.

Armour: 800
Shields: 1200

A heavy-weight fighter optimised for maximum durability and fire-power at the cost of maneuverability. Has a central gun with a low rate of fire, slow projectile speed, but very high damage, as well as two faster weapons mounted on either side of the front-center.

Armour: 1200
Shields: 1600
Top Speed: 500 m/s
Weaponry (rpm, muzzle vel., damage):
